Courses section

Web development

Web development blends technical skills with creative problem-solving. It's the process of creating and maintaining websites and web applications. The primary goal is to build functionality to ensure users can interact with digital content in a smooth and efficient way.

Mobile development

Mobile development is the process of creating software applications for mobile devices like smartphones and tablets. This involves a full lifecycle of design, coding, and testing, with developers choosing between creating native apps specific to a platform like iOS or Android, or cross-platform apps that can run on multiple systems.

Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, damage, or unauthorized access. It involves using technologies, processes, and policies to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information in an increasingly connected world.

Data science

Data science combines math and statistics, specialized programming, advanced analytics, artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning with specific subject matter expertise to uncover actionable insights hidden in an organization's data.

About section

TechLaunch is a technology accelerator and a training provider that helps early-stage startups secure funding and prepares individuals for IT careers. While originally an accelerator for tech startups, its online training arm, TechLaunch Academy, focuses on preparing individuals for IT and cybersecurity roles through intensive, self-paced online bootcamps. Currently, the training programs are no longer active but its career services are still available for past students.
